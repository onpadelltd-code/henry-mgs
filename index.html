Year 4 form tutors: Miss Atty and Mrs Callaghan. Parents meet form tutors at the Curriculum Briefing on Thu 10 Sep 2026.

🚗 Parking — Rectory Gate
  • Park around Danes Road / Brighton Grove (postcode M14 5JT)
  • Walk via the footpath to the MGS Rectory Gate, then to school
  • Gate open 7:50–9:15 am every morning
  • Gate open 3:20–4:05 pm every afternoon
  • Park considerately — avoid the busy junction and the cul-de-sac

See Welcome Pack p.31. On-site parking is very limited. For special events (e.g. New Starter Afternoon) the gate may open at different times — check the event notes.

🌅 Wraparound Care
  • Runs 8:00 am – before school, and after school until 6:00 pm
  • No charge, no booking required
  • After-school session starts with homework time
  • Light snack provided in the afternoon — breakfast at home in the morning
  • Must be dropped off and collected by an adult
  • £15 per 15-minute period charged after 6:00 pm
👔 Junior School Uniform
  • Dark blue blazer with Junior School badge
  • School tie (Junior School design)
  • White shirt — long sleeve winter, short sleeve summer
  • Trousers — plain dark grey or black (conventional cut). Grey school shorts above the knee allowed
  • Jumper (optional) — V-neck navy, with or without owl motif. No sweatshirts
  • Shoes — plain black leather, polished. No trainers, no logos
  • Plain grey socks
  • Outdoor wear — raincoat / cagoule / anorak; plain dark accessories (no football scarves)
  • Two bags — one for books, one for sportswear

Supplier: Monkhouse (stores in Altrincham, Cheadle, Bury). All items must be labelled. Parents' Society holds second-hand sales — first one 19 June 2026.

⚽ PE & Games Kit — Compulsory

From Monkhouse:

  • Reversible games jersey
  • Long socks
  • Shorts
  • Crew-neck T-shirt

From any high-street store:

  • Gym shoes / clean trainers (indoor)
  • Second pair of training shoes (outdoor, ideally for astro)
  • White sports socks (indoor PE)
  • Plain black/navy swimming trunks (tight-fitting)
  • Swimming goggles
  • Shin pads — rugby & football
  • Studded boots — rugby & football
  • Mouth guard (highly recommended — see OPRO dates below)

Wednesday is Year 4 Sport day — full kit needed. Optional Castore branded items available; only buy if Henry joins a team.

✏️ Other Equipment
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Blue handwriting pen (not biro)
  • Green pen
  • H or 2H pencils, sharpener, rubber
  • Small set of coloured pencils
  • 30 cm ruler
  • Basic geometry set
  • Glue stick
  • Safety scissors
  • Pocket Oxford English Dictionary

Label everything with permanent name labels.

🦷 OPRO Mouthguard Fittings
  • Thursday 3 September · 11:00 – 14:00
  • Monday 7 September · 10:00 – 14:30

Strongly recommended — all boys play rugby/hockey. Order ahead at oproschools.com. Prices from £41.99. Includes complimentary Snap-Fit while waiting.

📚 Homework — Year 4
  • 20–30 minutes per day plus reading, spelling, mental maths
  • No new homework set on Wednesdays — catch-up day for reading and projects
  • Homework recorded in independent learning planner
  • If too difficult after a reasonable attempt — note from home
  • Times Tables Rock Stars & MyMaths encouraged daily
